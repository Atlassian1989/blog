VIM Cheatsheet
==============


Window Splits
=============
Vertical Split : Ctrl+w + v
Horizontal Split: Ctrl+w + s
Close current windows: Ctrl+w + q

Paste
=========
:set paste
Paste content
:set nopaste

Insert new line 
===============
o

Undo changes
============
:undolist
g- to 'go back in time'
g+ to 'go forward in time'
